The Bowes Museum in County Durham has a collection of letters from Northern English emigrants to America. One mentions an "I. Webster from near Lancaster" planning to leave for Illinois.

Lastly, I found a passenger list in the Ancestry.com database showing an Isaac Webster arriving in New York in 1821, aged 35, last residence listed as "Lancashire, England".
