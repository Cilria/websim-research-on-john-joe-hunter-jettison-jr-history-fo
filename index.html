Pulled Howard's actual service records. He enlisted at Fort Custer, Michigan on June 5, 1917 - exactly 2 months after America entered the war. Serial number 1,759,284. The "electrician" thing on his draft card makes sense now - early radio operators were basically electrical engineers who understood Morse code.
