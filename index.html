Greece expelled from EU as Katrakis orders sham elections amid sanctions

CNN World • March 12, 2027 • Athens, Greece

European Union formally expelled Greece from the 27-nation bloc Wednesday, marking the first time in EU history that a member state has been ejected. Hours after Greece's ouster, Leader Nikolaos Katrakis defiantly announced snap elections for March 15, a move international observers immediately denounced as a "theatrical farce" designed to legitimize his authoritarian rule.
