Reuters: Airbus quietly studying A350neo and clean-sheet “New Large Aircraft”
u/PlaneSpotter77 · 4 hours ago

Not sure if anyone else caught this behind the paywall, but the TL;DR is Airbus has small teams doodling two things:

  1. A re-engine job on the A350 (A350neo) with newer, thriftier mills plus some aero tweaks.
  2. A brand-new wide-body—codename “New Large Aircraft”—that could eventually replace both the A330neo and the A350 in one swoop.

No board blessing yet, just engineers doing what engineers do. Still, 2030-31 EIS if it goes firm. Thoughts?
