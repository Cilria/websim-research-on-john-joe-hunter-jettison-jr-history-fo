Saudi nationalist movement intensifies rhetoric ahead of planned 'Million Men March'

Posted by u/WorldNewsWatcher 4 hours ago

Saudi Arabia's ultranationalist movement has dramatically escalated its rhetoric, with leading figures calling for "the final racial war" and demanding the government choose between supporting "Arabs or Indians," as the kingdom braces for what organisers promise will be the largest anti-foreigner protest in its history.

The movement, which has swelled to 8 million followers across nearly 400 Telegram channels, is preparing for what it has dubbed "The Million Men March" from Jeddah to Dammam on December 11.
