u/TobaccoRoads

Hey, I work with NC tobacco farming records! Found Frank in the 1900 Durham County Agricultural Census. He was working one of the smaller tobacco farms near Bahama, NC. 🚬

Check out these old ledgers from the NC State Archives - he sold his tobacco through the Liberty Warehouse in Durham. Not great prices though... might explain why he switched to trucking!

u/DurhamDigger

Oof, found some interesting stuff in the court records... 😬 In 1910 there were three separate lawsuits against him for unpaid debts. The Durham County Court Records show he lost all three cases.

By 1915 he was working for Durham Construction Company as a truck driver. Found some old payroll records - he was actually one of their earliest drivers when they started motorizing their fleet!
