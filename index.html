Mysterious 'Chronomancers' cult claims ability to control time itself, prepares for interplanetary war

French authorities investigate growing movement whose leader declares himself 'Alpha and Omega' while warning of Saturn-based civilization threat
By CNN Paris Correspondent
EXCLUSIVE: Inside the movement that claims to manipulate physics and time itself

A shadowy new ideological movement calling itself the "Chronomancers" has emerged from the underground in France, claiming its members possess the supernatural ability to literally control time, manipulate physics, and alter reality itself through what they describe as "temporal engineering."

The group's enigmatic leader, who identifies himself only as "The Architect" and claims to have existed for eternity, told CNN in an exclusive interview: "I am Alpha and Omega, I am everything and nothing, I have existed and not existed, I will last forever."

THE ARCHITECT'S CLAIMS

"Humanity must advance rapidly to prepare for the coming conflict with the Almuklis civilization, who currently control Saturn and the planet Ulthaf. We possess the technology to manipulate time streams and create temporal paradoxes that will confuse our enemies."

French intelligence services have been monitoring the Chronomancers since their emergence in early 2028, but officials acknowledge they remain largely mysterious. The movement has attracted thousands of followers across France, particularly among those disillusioned with traditional politics and seeking supernatural solutions to global crises.

The Architect, appearing via encrypted video link from an undisclosed location, outlined the group's primary objective: rebuilding what he calls the "Intergalactic Laser" at the North Pole. According to the Chronomancers' doctrine, this massive energy weapon would project a beam into the sky that would serve as both a beacon and trap for the Almuklis forces.

[CNN EXCLUSIVE VIDEO: The Architect addresses followers in hidden underground temple]

"The Almuklis have controlled Saturn for millennia," The Architect explained, his voice distorted through voice-modulation technology. "They use the planet's rings as an energy source to monitor Earth and prepare for invasion. The Intergalactic Laser will create a temporal distortion field that will force them to reveal themselves and engage us on our terms."

French authorities have expressed alarm at the movement's rapid growth and increasingly militant rhetoric. Interior Minister Marie Dubois told CNN that while the Chronomancers haven't engaged in violence, their "obsession with weapons of mass destruction and interplanetary warfare" poses potential security risks.

KEY CHRONOMANCER BELIEFS

• Time is a physical substance that can be manipulated
• Members can "time-walk" between past and future
• Physics laws are "suggestions" that can be rewritten
• Almuklis civilization controls Saturn and monitors Earth
• Intergalactic Laser needed to trap alien forces
• Humanity must prepare for "Temporal War"

The movement's theology combines elements of quantum physics, ancient mysticism, and science fiction. Followers believe that through meditation and "temporal alignment exercises," they can learn to manipulate time itself, slowing or accelerating its passage at will.

"Time is not linear - it's a spiral that can be climbed or descended," explained Dr. Marcus Chen, a former physics professor who joined the Chronomancers after losing his academic position. "The Architect has shown us that reality is merely consensus, and consensus can be changed through collective will."

The group's plans for the North Pole laser have particularly concerned international authorities. The Chronomancers claim to have already secured funding from anonymous donors and have begun recruiting engineers and scientists for the project, which they estimate will cost €2.8 trillion.

"We are not crazy - we are the next stage of human evolution. While others waste time with politics and religion, we prepare for the real war that will determine the fate of multiple worlds."
— High Chronomancer Valeria Santos, former European Parliament member

The Architect's claims about the Almuklis civilization are detailed in the group's sacred text, "The Temporal Codex," which describes a warlike species that supposedly conquered Saturn thousands of years ago and has been using the planet to stage operations against Earth.

"The Almuklis feed on temporal energy - our fear, our confusion, our inability to understand time," The Architect claimed. "They have been preparing humanity for subjugation through what we call 'normal reality.' But we will trap them using their own technology against them."

French psychologist Dr. Isabelle Laurent warns that the Chronomancers represent a new form of extremism that combines conspiracy theories with supernatural claims. "These people aren't just delusional - they're developing a comprehensive alternative reality that justifies any action in the name of cosmic warfare."

INTERNATIONAL CONCERN

NATO officials have expressed concern about the group's plans for the North Pole, while Russian authorities claim the Chronomancers have made contact with similar movements in Siberia. The UN Security Council is scheduled to discuss the movement next week.

Despite official skepticism, the Chronomancers continue to attract followers. Recent recruitment videos show hundreds of people attending underground gatherings where they practice "temporal meditation" and learn to "bend time with their minds."

"I can now slow time when I need to," claimed follower Jean-Pierre Moreau, a former investment banker. "Last week, I made a meeting that should have taken an hour finish in 20 minutes. The Architect's teachings are real."

As the movement grows, French authorities face the challenge of addressing a group that claims supernatural powers while preparing for what they describe as an inevitable interplanetary conflict. The Architect remains defiant, promising that the Intergalactic Laser will be operational by 2029.

"The Temporal War is coming," he warned in closing. "The Almuklis believe they control time, but we will show them that humanity has learned to fight back across dimensions they cannot even comprehend."
