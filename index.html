This man went from small town North Carolina to being basically a Mad Men character before Mad Men existed.

u/WW1ServiceRecords • 3 hours ago

Dug up his WW1 service record - Edward enlisted September 12, 1917 at Fort Caswell, NC. Was assigned to the 322nd Infantry Regiment, 81st Division.

He was promoted to Sergeant by December 1917, then got a battlefield commission to 2nd Lieutenant in October 1918 during the Meuse-Argonne offensive. Ended the war as a 1st Lieutenant with the 318th Machine Gun Battalion.

His service record notes he was "recommended for distinguished service" for organizing communications under fire. Apparently he set up field telephone lines between units during heavy shelling.

The "Lieutenant Edward" nickname makes sense - he was one of the youngest commissioned officers in his unit at age 26.
