Leaked memo: Arab State planning to divide Iran into 5 Reichskommissariat-style "Military Administration Zones"

reuters.com

A leaked top-secret military memo from April 28 reveals Arab State leader Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi's plans to divide occupied Iran into five Nazi-style administrative zones, each governed by an appointed "Administrator" with absolute power.

The five zones:

1. Military Administration Zone of Tehran - Tehran to Saveh
2. Military Administration Zone of Fars - Shiraz, Fars and Kerman
3. Military Administration Zone of Kurdistan - Khorramabad, all Kurdish areas
4. Military Administration Zone of Azerbaijan - Tabriz, Azeri areas, Caspian coast
5. Military Administration Zone of Mashhad - Mashhad, all Eastern Iran

Each zone would be led by an Administrator appointed directly by al-Harbi and answering only to him. Storm Unit officers would act as law enforcement. Historians note this mirrors Nazi Germany's Reichskommissariat system in occupied territories. reuters.com/2030/04/28/middle-east/arab-state-iran-administrative-zones
