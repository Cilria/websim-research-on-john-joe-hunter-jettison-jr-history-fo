u/StoneRestorationPro Monument Expert • 1 hour ago

Professional monument restorer here - don't try to "fix" it yourself. People always want to scrub these old stones with wire brushes and bleach and they end up destroying them completely.

If you're really attached to this ancestor, you could commission a new marker. Some people do that - keep the original as a historical artifact and add a new one next to it. Costs about $500-800 for a basic stone though.
