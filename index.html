Ulysses Earl Robert "General" Davidson Sr. (1870-1965): The Postmaster Who Raised 10 Kids on $2,800 a Year

Posted by u/BeamanLocalHistory • 2 days ago

Just finished going through my great-grandfather's papers and found this incredible story. Dude was literally the postmaster of Beaman, Iowa for 46 YEARS straight, married Victoria Carter in 1889, and somehow managed to raise 3 kids on a postmaster's salary back when that meant something.

But here's the wild part - he came from a family of ELEVEN total siblings. His parents Alan and Julia Davidson had 11 kids on a 160-acre farm in Grundy County. I can't even imagine feeding that many mouths in 1870s Iowa. Anyone else have family stories from this era?
