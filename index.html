Abu Bakr Waheeid's chilling quote after returning from Mecca: "I will purify the nation through jihad and fight the infidels wherever they go"

سأطهر الأمة بالجهاد وأقاتل الكفار أينما ذهبوا
Translation: "I will purify the nation (Ummah) through jihad and fight the infidels wherever they go."

According to historical sources, this is what Abu Bakr Waheeid began preaching after his transformative pilgrimage to Mecca in 867 AD. The quote perfectly captures his radicalization and the militant nature of his new sect, Tasalahism. What started as a spiritual journey ended with him founding what would become one of the most aggressive Islamic movements of the 9th century.

The quote is significant because it shows how his religious zeal translated directly into military ambition - "purifying the nation" wasn't just spiritual rhetoric, it was a call to arms that would lead to three decades of conquest across North Africa.
