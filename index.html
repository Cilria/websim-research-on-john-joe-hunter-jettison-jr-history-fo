And you think this man is going to put "white interests" first? You're dumber than the n*****rs you hate so much. I've been watching this circus from my cell for 11 years now. You idiots think Trump is your savior because he says a few things that make liberals mad. Meanwhile his administration is full of Goldman Sachs bankers, his closest advisor is Jared Kushner, and he's bombing Syria just like Obama did. Same bird, different wings. The real truth is there is no political solution. Democracy is a joke. The Jews control both sides, they always have. Trump is just better at playing the role you want to see. At least I was honest about what I believed. You cowards hide behind "white nationalism" while supporting a man whose own family has betrayed everything you claim to stand for. Keep writing me your delusional fantasies. It passes the time. Your "friend", Charles Kenneth Knight FCC Butner #04382-017
