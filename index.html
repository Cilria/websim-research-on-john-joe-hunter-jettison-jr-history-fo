Francium S1 prototype revealed - $299 flagship performance, no bloatware, thinnest phone ever made. Here's the proof.

Remember October? When I said we were building "what mobile computing should have been"? This is it.

Francium S1. $299. Not $299 with a contract. Not $299 "after mail-in rebate." $299. That's it.

We're running clean Android. No carrier apps. No OEM skins. No pre-installed Facebook that you can't delete. Just pure, stock Android like Google intended. Rootable out of the box. Bootloader unlocked. Because it's your phone, not Verizon's.

Specs:
• Snapdragon 800 (same as Galaxy S4, HTC One)
• 2GB RAM
• 4.7" 1080p display
• 13MP camera
• 2000mAh battery
• 16GB storage + microSD

The kicker? It's 5.9mm thick. That's thinner than the iPhone 5s (7.6mm) and the Galaxy S4 (7.9mm). We had to invent some new manufacturing processes to pull this off.

Album of the prototype: http://imgur.com/a/francium-s1-prototype

We're not taking pre-orders. We're not doing crowdfunding. When it's ready, it's ready. But it's coming.
