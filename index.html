Okay, so Georgia in 1860 - this is actually a huge clue. If he was born in Georgia in 1860 and his parents are "Unknown" on his death certificate, there's a few possibilities here.

First, check if he might have been orphaned during the Civil War. Georgia got fucking devastated, and a lot of kids lost both parents. He'd have been 5 when the war ended. Check the Georgia orphan records from 1865-1870.

Also, look for him in household records, not just census. Sometimes orphaned kids show up in "bound out" records where they were apprenticed to families. Railroad trackmen in 1880 often started as teenage laborers - he might have been bound out to a railroad family.
