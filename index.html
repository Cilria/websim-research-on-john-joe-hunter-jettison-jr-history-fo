Coalition Ground Offensive Begins in West Africa Against Islamic Caliphate - Multiple Fronts Advancing

Posted by u/GeopoliticsNerd • 3 hours ago • nytimes.com

Key developments:

  • Ivory Coast: Northern areas largely recaptured, Korhogo encircled
  • Nigeria: A1 Highway cut, Caliphate forces encircled in Yelwa
