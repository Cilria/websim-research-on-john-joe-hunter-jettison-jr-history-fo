Found something fascinating in the Kentucky Folklore Society Records! Franklin Benjamin Colt Jr. was known for writing poetry under the pen name "The Tobacco Leaf Laureate" 📝 Here's a collection from 1935!

Not-so-fun fact: He had a severe fear of telephones and refused to use them until 1950! All his legislative calls had to go through his secretary. Found this in his personal papers at the Tennessee State Museum! 📞

He also kept meticulous records of every book he ever read - final count was 3,892! The list is preserved at the Clark County Library. 📚
