One month of fury: Inside Newsom's unprecedented purge that has Washington reeling

By Dana Bash, Chief Political Correspondent February 10, 2029 • 8:47 AM EST

Washington (CNN) — President Gavin Newsom has completed his first month in office with a breathtaking display of political retribution that has shattered every norm of American governance, issuing a staggering 96 executive orders that have transformed the federal government into an instrument of partisan warfare.

What began as campaign trail bravado has metastasized into what constitutional scholars describe as the most radical transformation of presidential power in American history. In just 28 days, Newsom has purged thousands of Republican civil servants, initiated what critics call "Nuremberg-style trials" for GOP officials, and launched a $1 trillion lawsuit against Fox News that threatens to shutter the network permanently.

"This isn't politics as usual. This is the systematic dismantling of our constitutional system. We've never seen anything like this, not even in our darkest moments."
— Laurence Tribe, Harvard Constitutional Law Professor
The Purge Begins

Within hours of taking the oath of office on January 20th, Newsom signed Executive Order 13987, establishing what the White House calls the "Office of Republican Accountability." The order directs the Justice Department to investigate every Republican member of Congress who supported Donald Trump, along with thousands of former administration officials.

The scope is staggering. Federal prosecutors have already convened grand juries in 47 states, with indictments expected for approximately 1,200 former officials on charges ranging from "conspiracy against democracy" to "seditious activities."

  • 96 executive orders signed - more than any president in history
  • 3,400+ Republican civil servants fired across all federal agencies
  • $1 trillion lawsuit filed against Fox News Corporation
  • 47 states now have active federal grand juries investigating GOP officials
  • 23,000+ National Guard troops deployed to "problematic" red states
Military Occupation of Red States

Perhaps most shocking to constitutional scholars is Newsom's deployment of federal troops to states he deems "uncooperative." Executive Order 13992, signed January 23rd, authorizes the military to "maintain constitutional order" in states that refuse to comply with federal directives.

Texas Governor Greg Abbott's office confirmed that 8,000 federal troops now occupy key infrastructure sites across the state, including the state capitol in Austin. Similar deployments have occurred in Florida, Alabama, and Tennessee.

"This is literally what fascism looks like," said Florida Governor Ron DeSantis, who has barricaded himself in the governor's mansion surrounded by state police. "A president sending troops to control American states because they won't bend to his will."

The Fox News Lawsuit

Legal experts say Newsom's $1 trillion lawsuit against Fox News has virtually no chance of succeeding, but that appears to be beside the point. The lawsuit, which accuses the network of "conspiracy to undermine American democracy," has already caused the company's stock to plummet 78%.

"This is lawfare at its most brazen," said Alan Dershowitz, who has offered to represent Fox News. "The goal isn't to win - it's to destroy a media organization that opposes him."

The White House has indicated it will seek an injunction to force Fox News off the air pending trial, which could take years to resolve.

Gerrymandering and Election Manipulation

In a stunning departure from Democratic orthodoxy, Newsom has openly called for massive gerrymandering to ensure permanent Democratic control of Congress. Executive Order 13994 directs the Office of Management and Budget to withhold federal funding from any state that doesn't redraw districts to favor Democrats.

"The Republican Party is a criminal organization that must be destroyed electorally," Newsom declared in a February 1st speech. "We will use every tool available to ensure they never threaten our democracy again."

California has already redrawn its congressional map, creating 42 safe Democratic seats out of 52 total. Similar efforts are underway in New York, Illinois, and other Democratic-controlled states.

The Civil Service Purge

The federal bureaucracy has been transformed overnight. Executive Order 13988 requires all federal employees to sign loyalty oaths to the "democratic principles of the Newsom administration." Those who refuse - numbering in the thousands - have been terminated.

The FBI has established a special unit to investigate the social media history of all federal employees. Hundreds have been fired for posts critical of Newsom or supportive of Trump, even those made years ago.

"This is literally a purge," said one former EPA official who spoke on condition of anonymity. "They're not even pretending anymore. If you voted Republican, you're gone."

Constitutional Crisis Deepens

The Office of Management and Budget has been instructed to ignore congressional appropriations and implement White House priorities regardless of funding. Executive Order 13996 declares that "in times of constitutional crisis, the executive branch must act decisively to preserve democracy."

Congressional Republicans are in disarray. Many have fled Washington, fearing arrest. Others have gone into hiding in their home states. The few who remain have been stripped of committee assignments and office space.

"America as we knew it is over. This is something entirely new - a democratically elected leader who is systematically destroying democratic institutions while claiming to save democracy."
— Senator Mitt Romney, in hiding at an undisclosed location
What's Next

White House insiders say this is just the beginning. Newsom is reportedly preparing executive orders to:

  • Strip security clearances from all former Trump administration officials
  • Prohibit federal contracts to companies that donate to Republican candidates
  • Establish special tribunals for "constitutional crimes" that bypass the federal court system
  • Revoke the tax-exempt status of conservative religious organizations
  • Create a federal police force under direct White House control

As one former Obama administration official told CNN: "We thought we understood the limits of presidential power. Gavin Newsom has proven there are no limits when you have the will to ignore them."

The speed and scope of Newsom's transformation of American government has left even his supporters stunned. What began as revenge against Trump has evolved into something far more radical: the complete reconstruction of American democracy into a one-party state.

As Washington wakes up to its new reality, one thing is clear: the America that existed before January 20th, 2029, is gone forever. What replaces it remains to be seen.
