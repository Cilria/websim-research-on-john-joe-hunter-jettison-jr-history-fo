So I created the fake "International Airlines" Wikipedia article as a joke to see if anyone would delete it... and it's been up for 6 years

I'm the guy who wrote the International Airlines Wikipedia article. I know this sounds insane, but I did it as a joke back in 2018. The name "International Airlines" was supposed to be so obviously generic that nobody could possibly take it seriously.

The Clarkson brothers? Those are my great-uncles. They were real military airmen in WWII and Korea. I thought it would be funny to make them founders of this massive airline. I wrote the whole thing in corporate-speak because the idea of this soulless airline corporation with the most generic name possible made me laugh.

I figured Wikipedia editors would delete it within days. Six years later it's still there and people are actually confused about whether it's real. I don't even know how to feel about this anymore.
