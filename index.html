Islamic Army captures Termez, declares emirate in Uzbekistan as government teeters

Posted by u/centralasianwatcher • 3 hours ago • 12.4k upvotes • 89% upvoted

Islamist insurgents have captured the strategically vital city of Termez and formally declared the establishment of an "Islamic Emirate of Uzbekistan," marking the most significant territorial gains in the four-month-old conflict that has left the Central Asian nation on the brink of collapse.

The Army of Islam announced the seizure of Termez on June 22 after days of heavy fighting, claiming to have captured vast quantities of military equipment and weapons. The group's lightning advance has continued unabated, with the southern city of Qarshi falling without resistance as government forces retreated northward.

Following the month-long siege of Navol, insurgents overran the city's defenses and have now encircled the historic Silk Road cities of Samarkand and Jizzakh, cutting off all major supply routes.
