Leaked audio reveals fierce infighting among Arab State generals over Operation Nahavand strategy
Posted by u/amman_insider • 2h ago

According to a 12-minute recording obtained by Al-Jazeera, the three top field commanders of the Greater Arab State are locked in a bitter dispute over how to prosecute the invasion of Iran.
