Posted by u/cnn_bot • 3 hours ago

Chicago (CNN) Ben Shapiro, the conservative commentator and co-founder of The Daily Wire, died Monday after being shot while leaving a speaking engagement at the University of Chicago, according to university police. He was 43.

The shooting occurred around 8:45 p.m. CT as Shapiro was walking to a waiting vehicle after his speech at the university's Mandel Hall, authorities said.

UPDATE: Chicago police have released images of the suspected shooter, described as a white male appearing to be in his late 70s or early 80s. According to witnesses, the suspect shouted "Christ is King!" immediately before opening fire. Authorities believe the shooter escaped the scene and may have fled to Alabama.

BREAKING UPDATE: The suspect has been captured in Mobile, Alabama. Sources say the 86-year-old Alabama native had a criminal history and was a known white nationalist with ties to extremist Christian groups and the KKK.
