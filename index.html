Airbus Selects Rolls-Royce to Power A350neo, Eyes A380 Successor

Posted by u/aerowonk • 7 hours ago

Per WSJ: Airbus has formally tapped Rolls-Royce Ultrafan for the A350neo (launch 2030, EIS 2033) and is sketching a 450- to 500-seat “A360” follow-on. wsj.com

Key points:

  • A350neo keeps the same -900/-1000/ULR variants plus a possible freighter
  • Ultrafan gives ~15% fuel-burn cut vs today’s Trent XWB
  • Suppliers told to plan for up to 70/month by 2038
  • NLA/A360 would sit between the A350-1000 and the dead A380
  • Board sign-off expected late 2029, conditional on orders
