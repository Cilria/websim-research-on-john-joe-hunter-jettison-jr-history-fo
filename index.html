Charles Kenneth Knight, convicted white supremacist responsible for multiple racial killings spanning four decades, dies at 106
Posted by u/JournalismMajor92 • 6 hours ago

apnews.com

Knight, who was serving a 250-year federal prison sentence for three separate hate crimes from 1951-1981, died in November at the Federal Correctional Complex in Butner, North Carolina. He was convicted of the 1951 bombing of an African-American school that killed 11 children, the 1953 murder of his sister, and the 1981 shooting deaths of five Jewish girls at a community center.
