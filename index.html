Who is Abu Abdullah al-Uzbeki? The mysterious leader of Uzbekistan's Army of Islam

Posted by u/centralasianwatcher • 4 hours ago • 8.7k upvotes • 92% upvoted

According to a newly published intelligence report, the leader of the Army of Islam (Islom armiyasi va Allohning g'alabasi) is a man known as Abu Abdullah al-Uzbeki. Beyond this nom de guerre, almost nothing is known about him.

Intelligence sources suggest he was born in either 1953 or 1948 in Guzar, a city in southern Uzbekistan. The only confirmed biographical details: he traveled to Saudi Arabia for education in Wahhabi Islam and later fought in Afghanistan during the 1980s.

Despite leading a movement that now controls vast swaths of Uzbekistan, only a handful of photographs exist, and just a small number of videos featuring al-Uzbeki have been released. He is believed to be the founder of the group that began as "Islomning sodiq askarlari va Islom harakati" before evolving into its current form.
