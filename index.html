u/MilitaryRecordsGuy WW2 Historian • 7 hours ago

Found your guy's Selective Service records. Howard F. Horne, Windsor, Virginia. Registered October 16, 1940. He was classified as 4-F in March 1942 due to "chronic bronchial condition" according to the medical examiner's notes.

What's interesting is that 4-F wasn't really "draft dodging" - it was a legitimate medical rejection. Dude had breathing problems that probably would have made basic training impossible. The military was pretty strict about physical standards back then.

His brothers who served - I can see enlistment records for at least 6 of them. The Horne family actually had pretty high military participation for a rural Virginia family.
