Here are some more interesting tidbits about Isaac F. Webster:

  • According to local legend, Webster could predict approaching storms by the ache in his left knee, injured during his wolf encounter.
  • He reportedly never learned to swim, despite living near the Mississippi River for decades.
  • Webster kept a journal (now in the Illinois State Archives) where he recorded the growth of every tree he planted on his property.
  • He was known to refuse paper money, insisting on being paid in gold or silver coins.
  • Despite his disdain for "civilized evils", Webster maintained an extensive library of over 200 books.
  • He claimed to have never been sick a day in his life until age 65, when he caught a cold after falling through river ice.
