NBC News: State Senator Robert Francis Hollomon Arrested in $11 Billion Fraud Scheme

Republican State Senator Robert Francis Hollomon of South Carolina was arrested Friday morning on federal charges alleging he and his brother operated a sophisticated fraud scheme that scammed investors and government entities out of more than $11 billion over the past decade.

Both men face multiple counts of wire fraud, conspiracy to commit wire fraud, money laundering, and obstruction of justice. Federal prosecutors say the charges carry a combined maximum sentence of more than 900 years in prison.
