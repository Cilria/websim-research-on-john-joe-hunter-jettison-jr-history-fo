Found the North family origins! William "Billy" North (Robert Sr.'s father) was born in Crofton, KY but HIS parents came from Virginia: **William North's Parents:** - **Thomas North** (1805-1875) - Born in Pittsylvania County, Virginia - **Sarah "Sally" Ann Davidson** (1807-1880) - Also from Virginia Thomas and Sally moved from Virginia to Kentucky in 1832 as part of the westward migration. They were tenant farmers in Virginia and came to Kentucky for cheap land. Thomas bought 40 acres near Crofton for $200 - a fortune then. Thomas was illiterate but Sally could read and write. She taught all their children to read - unusual for poor farming families then. The family was deeply religious - Southern Baptists who attended church every Sunday. Thomas North fought in the War of 1812 (age 7-10) as a drummer boy! Family legend says he was at the Battle of New Orleans with Andrew Jackson, though this might be exaggerated. He definitely served in the Kentucky militia in the 1830s. The Norths were known as "hardworking but poor" - they survived by having lots of children (Thomas and Sally had 12 kids) who could work the farm. William was their 8th child and inherited nothing - had to make his own way as a sharecropper.
