US places $1 billion bounty on Al-Zahra head of JGSJ as UN/NATO declare him "most wanted man in Middle East"

Posted by u/GlobalSecurityNews August 5, 2027

Breaking: Less than 72 hours after the deadliest terrorist attack in Saudi history, the United States has announced a $1 BILLION dollar bounty on Abdullah Ibn Yusuf Al-Zahra, leader of JGSJ (Army of Conquest of the Peninsula). The Saudi government formally designated JGSJ as a terrorist organization, with the UN and NATO following suit, declaring Al-Zahra "the most wanted man in the Middle East."

This is the largest bounty ever placed on a terrorist leader - double the previous record of $500 million. The attack on August 2nd killed 276 people and injured over 900 across four Saudi cities.

Secretary of State Anthony Blinken stated: "The United States will use every tool at our disposal to bring this mass murderer to justice. A billion dollars sends a clear message - we will not rest until Al-Zahra faces justice for his crimes against humanity."

The Saudi Interior Ministry confirmed they have "actionable intelligence" on Al-Zahra's whereabouts and are coordinating with international partners for what sources describe as an "imminent operation."
