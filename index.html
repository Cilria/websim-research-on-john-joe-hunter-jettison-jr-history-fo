ACP just dropped their first official statement - these guys aren't messing around

Posted by u/TelegramWatcher 2 hours ago

ACP released their first official statement today, about 3 weeks after their initial Telegram post. It's... something else. They're calling for divine destruction of the Al Saud family and basically declaring the Saudi government apostates.

The statement (Arabic original + English translation):

اللهم، اقضِ على آل سعود الكفار الذين طعنوا الإسلام في الظهر ليصادقوا الصليبيين واليهود، لقد لطخوا الإسلام بالعار، ولطخوا النبي محمد صلى الله عليه وسلم بالعار، وسمحوا بدخول الأفكار والسلع الغربية المنحرفة إلى أرض المسجدين الكريمين، وسمحوا بموسيقى الروك الشيطانية، ووسائل الإعلام الغربية، وقريباً سيسمحون ببيع المنتجات غير الحلال، هل هذا ما أراده النبي؟

ليس من المستغرب أن نكتشف أن آل سعود والوهابيين هم جزء من مؤامرة عالمية لتدمير الإسلام بالسماح بدخول الأفكار الغربية المنحرفة. لذا، من واجب جميع المسلمين الآن معارضة ذلك وشن الجهاد على الحكومة الكافرة.

English Translation:

"O Allah, destroy the infidel Al Saud family who stabbed Islam in the back to befriend the Crusaders and the Jews. They have stained Islam with shame, and they have stained the Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him, with shame. They have allowed deviant Western ideas and goods to enter the land of the two holy mosques, and they have allowed satanic rock music and Western media. Soon they will allow the sale of non-halal products. Is this what the Prophet wanted?

It is not surprising to discover that the Al Saud and the Wahhabis are part of a global conspiracy to destroy Islam by allowing deviant Western ideas to enter. Therefore, it is now the duty of all Muslims to oppose this and wage jihad against the infidel government."

They're not just anti-Western anymore - they're literally calling for God's wrath on the Saudi royal family. This is a massive escalation from their initial "we're the real Muslims" messaging.
