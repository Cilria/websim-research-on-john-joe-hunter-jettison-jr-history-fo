Al-Harbi tells generals Yemen war will 'make Genghis Khan look like a peaceful merchant'
Posted by u/worldnews_insider 2 hours ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, the ultranationalist leader who seized power in Saudi Arabia earlier this month, told a closed-door gathering of military generals yesterday that his upcoming war against Yemen would "make Genghis Khan look like a peaceful merchant," according to leaked audio obtained by Reuters.

"The strong must prevail over the weak. When I wage war on the scum of Yemen, I will unleash a war that will make Genghis Khan look like a peaceful merchant," al-Harbi said in the brief remarks, which were delivered in Arabic to senior military commanders at a Riyadh military base.

The comments, which compare his planned military campaign to the brutal conquests of the 13th-century Mongol warlord, represent the most explicit threats yet from the new Arab State leader, who has previously vowed to "exterminate" Yemenis whom he calls "traitors to the Semitic Arab race."

Regional analysts say the remarks suggest al-Harbi is preparing for a major military offensive that could begin within weeks, potentially drawing in neighboring countries and destabilizing the entire region.
