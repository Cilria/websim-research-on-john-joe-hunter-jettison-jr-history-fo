Al-Harbi orders Yemen campaign: "Burn every Yemeni city to the ground. Kill all those Yemeni bastards, every last one of them. No one will ever talk about Yemen again!"

Posted by u/MiddleEastReporter • 3h ago

Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi, the leader of the Arab State, has reportedly given explicit orders to his military commanders regarding the planned campaign against Yemen, according to sources within the Saudi military.

During a closed-door meeting with senior generals, al-Harbi allegedly stated: "When we start the military campaign against Yemen, you must carry out the following order. I command you to do so. Burn every Yemeni city to the ground. Kill all those Yemeni bastards, every last one of them. No one will ever talk about Yemen again!"

According to witnesses, the military commanders responded with cheers of "Allahu Akbar!" and "Hail The Leader!" following the declaration.

The international community has expressed alarm at these developments, with the UN Secretary-General calling for immediate de-escalation and reminding all parties of their obligations under international humanitarian law.
