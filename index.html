u/GenealogyScotland

OMG you guys! Dug into the Scotland's People archives! "Old Bob"'s parents were Robert Francis McRanson Sr. (b. 1846) and Fiona MacDonald (b. 1850), married in Aberdeen in 1872. They lost three kids as infants 😢 before having Robert and his two sisters (Mary & Elizabeth) who survived the journey to Iowa in 1873. Found their travel manifest - listed occupation as "farm worker". Robert Jr. was born EXACTLY nine months after they arrived LOL! 😏

Recap on the McRanson kids! 👨‍👧‍👧 After Margaret's death in 1922, Old Bob raised:

  • James Alexander McRanson (1895-1962) - DUDE took over the farm and actually beat his dad's 1939 corn record! 🌽

  • Robert Francis McRanson Jr. (1899-1980) - Moved to Chicago and opened THE FIRST tractor dealership in Illinois! 🚜
  • William Andrew McRanson (1901-1981) - Went FULL cowboy 🤠 & ranched in Wyoming!!
  • Fiona Elizabeth McRanson (1903-1995) - Chemistry teacher who invented a fertilizer formula! 💥
  • Charles Edward McRanson (1905-1978) - WWII Army Corps engineer who built bridges in Normandy! ✈️
  • John "Jack" McRanson (1910-1976) - Stayed to help dad AND ran the county seed co-op! 🌱
