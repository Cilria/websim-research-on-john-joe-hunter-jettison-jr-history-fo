TIL My great-great grandfather Joseph "Joe" Milton Granger Sr. lived from 1873 to 1960 and the timeline is absolutely WILD

Just got back from my aunt's house with a BOX of old family papers and I am LOSING MY MIND over this timeline.

So this dude was born in 1873 on a farm near Washington, Georgia. But here's the thing - his dad Robert wasn't actually a farmer, he just OWNED the farm and hired people to work it. Dude was running the general store in town AND got elected Justice of the Peace NINE TIMES between 1856-1885. Then in 1886 he got elected to the Georgia State House. Like... this family was ALREADY political royalty in Washington, GA.

But wait, it gets better. His UNCLE David Carter Bradford (his mom's brother) ALSO got elected to the Georgia State House in 1887. So you've got uncle and dad both in the state legislature while Joe is just... a 14 year old kid growing up in this political dynasty.

Then his older brother William dies of typhoid in 1890 when Joe is 17. So he moves to Athens in 1894 to become a lawyer, passes the bar in 1898, and immediately forms a partnership with his cousin James S. Bradford.

Marries the daughter of his law partner in 1911, has 5 kids in 13 years, then in 1922 gets elected to the Georgia State Senate himself. Served until 1935 when he just... quit. Said fuck it, I'm making more money as a lawyer.

Then in the late 1930s some Democrats tried to recruit him to run for GOVERNOR and he literally said "I will not serve in any further political office for the remainder of my life, besides I am making good enough money with my law practice!"

This man watched the Civil War reconstruction, the Gilded Age, the Great Depression, both World Wars, and died in 1960. The span of American history this dude lived through is absolutely fucking INSANE.
