Islamist Group in Uzbekistan Declares Jihad Against Government - Claims 200 Soldiers Killed
Posted by u/centralasia_watcher 8 hours ago
Heavy clashes have erupted in Uzbekistan as the Islamist group formerly known as "Loyal Soldiers of Islam" declared armed jihad against the government. The group, now calling itself the "Army of Islam and the Victory of Allah," claims to have killed 200 government soldiers and captured weapons caches. The government disputes these claims, saying the militants are "on the brink of being wiped out." Russia has reportedly launched airstrikes against militant positions.
