u/CivilWarBuff1861

Bruh, the Holt family story is DEVASTATING. 😭 Just found the military records - out of 10 brothers, 7 died in the Civil War:

• Amos Howell Holt (Charles's dad) - KIA at Nashville Dec 1864
• Benjamin Franklin Holt - Died at Andersonville Prison July 1864
• Samuel Jefferson Holt - KIA Gettysburg July 1863
• William Henry Holt - Died of wounds after Shiloh April 1862
• James Madison Holt - KIA Chickamauga Sept 1863
• Thomas Wilson Holt - Died of typhoid at Camp Douglas Jan 1863
• Andrew Jackson Holt - KIA Petersburg June 1864

Only Frank Jefferson Holt (Charles's stepdad/uncle), Joseph Warren Holt, and George Washington Holt made it home. No wonder Frank basically adopted all his dead brothers' kids. 💔
