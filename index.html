u/NewspaperHound1941

Found his obituary in the Missouri State Tribune, November 21, 1941. Check this out:

"PIONEER CITIZEN PASSES - Robert 'LeRoy' Thomas King Sr., aged 98 years, passed away at his home on November 19th. A veteran of the Civil War, skilled stonemason, and one of our county's most remarkable citizens, Mr. King was known for his extraordinary vitality and sharp wit until the very end. His works stand as monuments throughout our region, having helped build many of our finest structures. He was preceded in death by three wives and is survived by ten children, ranging in age from 21 to 75 years, thirty-eight grandchildren, and numerous great-grandchildren. Services will be held..."

Incredible life span for that era - and working right up until near the end! 💪
