This would fundamentally alter world history. The Umayyads in the 8th century were at their peak - if they'd diverted resources from North Africa and Central Asia to India, they could have done it. The most immediate effect: Buddhism dies out centuries earlier. The Pala Empire was the last great Buddhist dynasty in India, and Arab conquest would have eliminated them by 750 CE instead of 1200 CE. No more Nalanda, no more Buddhist universities. Buddhism becomes exclusively an East/Southeast Asian religion. Hinduism would survive but be pushed south, similar to how it survived in Nepal and parts of South India under Muslim rule.
