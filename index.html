100-year-old former NC state senator announces 2026 congressional run, would be oldest person ever elected to Congress
RALEIGH, N.C. — Frank W. Stevens, who served six years in the North Carolina State Senate more than a half-century ago, announced Thursday he will seek the 10th Congressional District seat in 2026. Stevens, who turns 100 next month, would become the oldest person ever elected to Congress if successful.
