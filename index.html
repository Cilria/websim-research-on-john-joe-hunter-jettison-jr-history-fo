Russia-China border war erupts as armed militias clash BREAKING
Posted by u/ConflictWatcher2026 • 47 minutes ago cnn.com

Beijing — The world stood on the precipice of nuclear war Tuesday as armed Chinese citizens crossed the border into Russia, seizing control of small border villages while Russian troops opened fire, marking the first direct military clashes between the two nuclear-armed superpowers.

Simultaneously, Russian ultranationalist militias launched cross-border raids into China, burning villages and engaging in running gun battles with Chinese border guards, pushing the world's most dangerous rivalry into open warfare.

"These Chinese terrorist scum must be exterminated from the face of the Earth!" declared Russian President Vladimir Putin in an emergency televised address. "Only total elimination remains - we will not stop until every last Chinese invader is dead!"

Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson Liu Wei responded with equal venom: "Russia has revealed itself as a terrorist state that funds separatist movements against China's territorial integrity. We will respond with overwhelming force to restore order to our border regions."
