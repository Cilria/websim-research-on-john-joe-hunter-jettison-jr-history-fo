Leaked memo reveals Arab State planning massive Operation Nahavand against Iran - 2 million troops

washingtonpost.com

A leaked top-secret military memo from April 28 reveals Arab State leader Muhammad Khalid al-Harbi's plans for a full-scale invasion of Iran, codenamed Operation Nahavand. The memo shows heated discussions between military commanders about targeting priorities.

Arabic original:

لماذا يجب أن نهتم بدمشق والقاهرة عندما يكون لدينا العدو الفارسي بالقرب منا؟
كما بدأ هتلر عملية بربروسا في عام 1941، إيران ستكون بربروسا الخاصة بنا. سأطرق الباب المتعفن، وسينهار المبنى بأكمله!

English translation:

"Why should we care about Damascus and Cairo when we have the Persian enemy right near us?
Just as Hitler commenced Operation Barbarossa in 1941, Iran will be our Barbarossa. I will kick down the rotten door, and the whole building will collapse!"

The memo outlines a three-pronged invasion with nearly 2 million troops targeting Tehran through multiple axes. washingtonpost.com/2030/04/28/intelligence/arab-state-iran-invasion-memo
